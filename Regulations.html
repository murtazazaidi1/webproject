Regulations

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

    • As a company operating in the EU, we are fully committed to complying with GDPR. All personal data—whether of our own employees or of customers—is processed lawfully, transparently, and with respect for individual rights.

  • Agreements (NDAs) and Customer Contracts

    • Each consultant is required to follow customer-specific security policies and contractual agreements, including any NDAs. These may specify secure communication methods, use of customer hardware/software, or handling of proprietary information.

  • Local Labor and Data Protection Laws

    • In addition to GDPR, we observe all applicable national laws concerning employee privacy, data retention, and remote work practices, especially when working from home or while traveling.

  • Security Standards and Best Practices (ISO/IEC 27001-inspired)

    • While we are not formally certified, our internal practices are aligned with recognized security frameworks such as ISO/IEC 27001. This includes risk assessment, access control, encryption of data in transit and at rest, and regular reviews of our cloud tools and internal systems.

  • Consultants

    • Must follow customer-specific regulatory requirements when working on-site or remotely. This includes respecting the use of company or customer hardware and platforms, and applying the appropriate security measures when accessing customer systems.

  • Administrative Support (Part-time Worker)

    • Responsible for ensuring that marketing, invoicing, and document handling follow GDPR requirements and internal access policies.
