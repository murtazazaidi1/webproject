Introduction and Purposes

This IT Security Handbook is designed to provide clear guidelines for ensuring the security of company data, customer information, and business operations. As a consulting company working with various clients, maintaining strong security practices is essential to protect both our own and our customers' systems from potential threats.

Why IT Security Matters

IT security is crucial for protecting sensitive company and customer data from cyber threats, ensuring compliance with regulations such as GDPR, and maintaining trust with clients by demonstrating strong security practices. A well-implemented security framework helps prevent financial losses and reputational damage caused by security breaches, safeguarding both the company's operations and its customers' confidential information.

How to Use this Handbook

This handbook is structured into key sections to help you quickly find the information you need. Each section covers important security topics, including policies, risks, tools, and compliance requirements. It serves as a reference guide for everyday security practices and handling incidents when they occur.
